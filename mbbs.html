Pharmacology

Pharmacology is the study of drugs and their effects on living organisms.

Anatomy

Anatomy is the study of the structure and organization of living things.

Biochemistry

Biochemistry is the study of the chemical processes that occur within living organisms.

Pathology

Pathology is the study of the causes and effects of diseases.

Microbiology

Microbiology involves the study of microorganisms such as bacteria, viruses, fungi, and parasites.

Physiology

Physiology focuses on the normal functions of living organisms and their parts.

Forensic Medicine

Forensic Medicine applies medical knowledge to legal issues and investigations.

Ophthalmology

Ophthalmology deals with the diagnosis and treatment of eye disorders.

Anesthesiology

Anesthesiology is concerned with the total perioperative care of patients before, during, and after surgery.

Dermatology

Dermatology focuses on diseases of the skin, hair, and nails in both children and adults.

Gynecology

Gynaecology is the area of medicine that involves the treatment of women's diseases, especially those of the reproductive organs.

Neurosurgery

Neurosurgery is the medical specialty concerned with the surgical treatment of disorders which affect any portion of the nervous system.

Orthopedics

Orthopedics is a branch of medicine that focuses on the care of the skeletal system and its interconnecting parts.

Cardiology

Cardiology is a branch of medicine that deals with disorders of the heart and the cardiovascular system.

Internal Medicine

Internal medicine is a medical specialty for medical doctors focused on the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of internal diseases in adults.
